How Campus Kartt Verification Works

Campus Kartt is not a marketplace. It is the trust layer that powers marketplaces and verified sellers.

01
🔍
Seller Identity Verification

Sellers submit KYC documents, shop registration, and operating history. Campus Kartt audits and approves before onboarding.

02
🪪
Product EcoID & Passport

Each product gets a unique EcoID and Digital Product Passport tracking condition, ownership history, and lifecycle data.

03
📡
Lifecycle Intelligence

Products are tracked across ownership cycles. Every resale updates the passport — so buyers always know a product's full story.
