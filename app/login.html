Problems We Solve

▶ Financial Waste
  • • High upfront cost of new academic equipment
  • • No reliable way to recover value by selling used items
  • • Significant financial burden on students each semester
▶ Environmental Waste
  • • Throwaway culture — usable items discarded after exams
  • • High carbon footprint from manufacturing new products
  • • No structured system to incentivise reuse on campus
▶ Logistical & Safety Issues
  • • Inefficient platforms like OLX and Facebook groups
  • • High risk of scams and fraudulent listings
  • • Privacy and safety concerns when meeting strangers

How It Works

01Sell your academic equipment or browse items listed by fellow students
02Buyer makes a secure payment through our verified platform
03On-campus agent facilitates handover — no personal contact details shared
04Every reused item reduces waste and saves carbon emissions
